"About"

Steppin' Stone is a one man band man hailing from Toronto, Canada. A dash of hope and a sprinkle of despair. Blues, soul and rock set to simmer in a pot. An experiment gone wrong, hearts not handled with care. the dead come back to life so, listen if you dare. Follow this ghoul on a musical journey as he creeps on creepin' on.
